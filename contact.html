Do you work with businesses outside of Wyoming?
+
-

Absolutely! While I'm based in Gillette, Wyoming, I work with businesses across the United States. Most of my work can be done remotely, and I use video calls for consultations and check-ins.

How quickly can you start working on my project?
+
-

It depends on the project scope and my current workload. For smaller projects like SEO audits or basic automation setups, I can usually start within a week. Larger projects like website development typically have a 2-3 week lead time.

Do you offer ongoing support after a project is completed?
+
-

Yes! I offer various ongoing support packages including monthly SEO maintenance, automation monitoring, and general tech support. I believe in building long-term relationships with my clients.

What if I'm not sure what I need?
+
-

No problem! That's exactly why I offer consultation calls. We'll discuss your business challenges, goals, and current setup to determine the best solutions for your specific needs and budget.

How do you handle project payments?
+
-

I typically work with a 50% deposit to start, with the remainder due upon completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. I accept checks, bank transfers, and major credit cards.
