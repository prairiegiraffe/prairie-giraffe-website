Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? Here are some of the most common ones I get from business owners like you.

Still have questions? Get in touch

Do you work with businesses outside of Wyoming?

Absolutely! While I'm based in Gillette, WY, I work with businesses across the United States. Most of my work can be done remotely, and I'm always happy to schedule video calls to discuss your project.

How quickly can you start working on my project?

It depends on my current workload and the scope of your project. For smaller projects, I can often start within a week. Larger projects may require 2-3 weeks lead time. I'll give you a clear timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you offer ongoing support after a project is completed?

Yes! I offer ongoing support packages for websites, SEO services, and automation systems. I believe in building long-term relationships and helping your business continue to grow.

What if I'm not sure what I need?

That's perfectly fine! I offer free consultations to help you identify your biggest opportunities. We'll discuss your current challenges and I'll recommend the best solutions for your specific situation.

How do you handle project payments?

For most projects, I require 50% upfront and 50% upon completion. For ongoing services like SEO, payments are monthly. I accept bank transfers, checks, and most major payment methods. All terms are clearly outlined in our agreement.
